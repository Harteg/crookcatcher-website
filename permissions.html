Permissions

Find Google Accounts on device

CrookCatcher needs this permission to find account candidates for setting up email.

Read, modify and delete the contents of your USB storage

CrookCatcher needs this permission to manage the pictures it takes.

Full network access

CrookCatcher needs this permission to use Google Maps and send emails.

Approximate location

CrookCatcher needs this permission to acquire the GPS coordinates of where pictures are taken.

Take pictures and video

CrookCatcher needs this permission to take pictures. To avoid any confusion: The app does not record video at any point, the permision for taking photos and recording videos is simply the same.
