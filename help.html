CrookCatcher

Help

How to uninstall CrookCatcher?

CrookCatcher needs Device Administrator Permission to work. Apps with Device Administrator Permission need to be deactivated before they can be uninstalled. So, simply open up CrookCatcher and deactivate it before uninstalling. If you run into issues, try restarting your phone, wait 5 minutes and try again.

Why does CrookCatcher need Device Administrator Permission?

The Device Administrator Permission is required for CrookCatcher to be notified about wrong unlock attempts. CrookCatcher will never delete any of your data, despite the system warning shown when activating.

Why does CrookCatcher stop working?

Your phone manufacturer might have imposed battery optimizing restrictions on CrookCatcher, preventing it from working in the background. To solve the issue, please follow instructions for your device on www.dontkillmyapp.com.

Why is CrookCatcher not detecting a wrong password?

If you are testing CrookCatcher, note that Android only detects a password or pattern as incorrect if it has at least 4 digits, characters, or pattern dots.

Can CrookCatcher detect wrong fingerprints?

No, Android sadly does not allow for CrookCatcher to detect wrong fingerprints.

Does CrookCatcher work with Face Unlock?

No, Android sadly does not allow for CrookCatcher to detect wrong faces.

Why does CrookCatcher not work with a pattern lock?

Some phones (e.g., some Xiaomi models) fail to transmit wrong unlock attempts to CrookCatcher when using a pattern lock. If CrookCatcher is not working, please try switching to a PIN or password lock.

Can I have CrookCatcher Pro on multiple devices?

Yes, if you purchase CrookCatcher Pro on one device, it will also be available on any other device that is signed into Google Play with the same Google account.

Why is CrookCatcher not working after a reboot?

Due to Android security rules, your phone must be unlocked once after reboot for CrookCatcher to access the camera. Unfortunately, this is a limitation that cannot be changed.

What should I do if I forgot my password, PIN code, or pattern lock?

The lock screen, and the password that is associated with it, is not a part of CrookCatcher. If you have forgotten your password, PIN code, or pattern, refer to your device's user manual.

Will CrookCatcher erase all my phone or tablet's data if too many incorrect passwords are typed?

No. CrookCatcher will never erase your device's data.

Why does CrookCatcher need access to my Gmail account?

Unlike other security applications, CrookCatcher does not rely on a third-party server for sending emails. Privacy is important, so CrookCatcher instead uses Google's authorization system OAuth2.0 to safely send emails directly from your device. This will not give CrookCatcher access to your email addresses or contacts. CrookCatcher will only use this permission to send security alert emails from your Gmail account to the email address defined by you from within the app. If you still feel uncomfortable with it, you can always create a new Gmail account just for CrookCatcher.

Can I get CrookCatcher for iPhone?

No, it is sadly not currently possible to build CrookCatcher for iPhone due to system limitations.
